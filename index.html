How to use

  • Rotary: Drag a hole to the stopper and release to dial a digit.
  • Keypad: Tap digits; DTMF tones play if Sound is on.
  • Keyboard: 0–9, Enter (call), Backspace (delete).
  • Contacts: Add and tap Call to auto-fill and call.
  • Settings: Switch theme, toggle sound/haptics, choose mode.
  • Offline: Install as app — works without internet.
